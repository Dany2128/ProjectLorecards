Galakrond - the father of dragons

He was the largest proto-dragon who had ever soared across Azeroth's skies. So mighty was the enormous creature that the beating of his wings could flatten entire forests." Galakrond was the largest proto-dragon of them all. With an insatiable hunger that actually drove him mad. Nothing could fill this beasts belly wich made him feast on his own felllow proto-drakes, slowly, turning him mad, almost corrupting his mind. Not long after he started to feast on his brethren he started growing necrotic limbs and dozens of old-god like eyes on his already afflicted form. Galakrond was defeated during a battle against the dragon aspects wich consisted of Malygos, Nozdormu, Neltharion, Alexstrasza and Ysera. After they defeated Galakrond, the aspects kept the truth about him a secret, hoping that noone will ever follow his path. In the World Of Warcraft expansion Wrath Of The Lich King you can travel to a zone called Dragonblight where you can take a look at Galakronds remains at "Galakrond's Rest".

Aman'thul - Highfather of the Pantheon

He was the first one to wake up, all alone, in search for the rest of his kin. Not knowing they existed but feeling that there has to be more out there. Aman'thul, the founding father of the pantheon, set out to discover the other nascent world-souls. Searching, jumping from world-soul to world-soul he slowly but surely awoken what would eventually become his siblings. Aman'thul and his siblings later on being known as The Pantheon, set out to stabilize the worlds. The sole purpose of the Pantheon was to bring order to the worlds. The Pantheon consisted of the following members: Aman'thul, Eonar, Norgannon, Golganneth, Aggramar and Sargeras.
